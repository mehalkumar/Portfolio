Bottle Design for a friend who suffers from epilepsy and prefers All-in-One Products. This design includes all her needs such as a medicine compartment, key holder, fidgety sections, and personalized name. A light bottle with a 24/7 cold water facility, which can also be converted into a cup, and a water measurer label inside.
